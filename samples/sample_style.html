    Fiddler Family Docs¶

    The page is under development

    These are the docs for everything that you could need as a member of the Fiddler Family, Fiddler Family Trust, or a member of The Statler.

    In these docs, you'll find information ranging from a "Watch / Read List" containing previously watched and read titles all the way to instructions for access IoT items on the Fiddler Family network at The Statler (and other properties).

    Additionally, there is information about managed business interests and managed websites.

    Legacy Instructions¶

    Start here if Evan or Hope has passed

    The Legacy Instructions walk you through, step-by-step, how to handle the logistics following a passing. Begin with Step 0: Prerequisites to establish email and password access — everything else depends on it.

    The Legacy guide covers, in order:

    1. Prerequisites — Email, passwords, and MFA access
    2. Home Infrastructure — Network, NAS, and connected devices
    3. Subscriptions — What to keep, pause, or cancel
    4. Business Interests — Invareo and managed entities
    5. Legal Documents — Wills, trusts, and legal processes
    6. Household Management — Day-to-day operations
    7. Financial Overview — Accounts and finances
    8. Digital Life — Online accounts and digital assets

    Otherwise, select the desired content from the navigation and proceed.
